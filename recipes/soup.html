Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This is homemade chicken soup the way my mom makes it, but is modified to be made with an instant pot instead of a stock pot. Makes delicious soup with just a few ingredients. Since I didn't want to make soup and take a picture of it just for this project, the photo was taken from the internet.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 large carrots
  • 4 stalks of celery
  • 1 onion
  • salt
  • noodles

Steps

  1. Place chicken in the instant pot
  2. chop up the carrots, celery, and onion and add to the instant pot
  3. Fill the instant pot with just enough water to cover the chicken and vegetables
  4. Close the instant pot and set for 20 minutes on high
  5. Instant release the pressure cooker
  6. Take out the chicken and let it cool a little before cutting it
  7. Cook the noodles according to package directions
  8. Cut the chicken and put the meat back in the soup
  9. Salt soup to taste
  10. Put noodles and soup in bowl and enjoy
